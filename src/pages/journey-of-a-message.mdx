## Journey of a Message

Communication is the process of transmitting information from one person to another. Throughout history, humans have continuously developed new ways to communicate faster, farther, and more reliably. What once took weeks or months can now happen in a matter of milliseconds.

In this exhibit, you will travel through six major eras of communication technology: handwritten letters carried by pigeons, early telephones, dial-up internet, fiber optic cables on the ocean floor, wireless networks, and what comes next. Along the way, you will see how each era solved the problems left behind by the one before it.
